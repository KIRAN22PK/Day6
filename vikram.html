ABOUT

Vikram is a 2022 Indian Tamil-language action thriller film directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj and produced by Raaj Kamal Films International. The film stars Kamal Haasan in the lead role, along with Vijay Sethupathi, Fahadh Faasil, and others in pivotal roles. The film's music is composed by Anirudh Ravichander.
