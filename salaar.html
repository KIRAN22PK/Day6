ABOUT

Salaar is an upcoming Indian Telugu-language action thriller film directed by Prashanth Neel. The film stars Prabhas in the lead role, with Shruti Haasan and Prithviraj Sukumaran in pivotal roles. The film's music is composed by Ravi Basrur.
