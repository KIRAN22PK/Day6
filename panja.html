ABOUT

Panja is a 2021 Indian Telugu-language action drama film directed by Sujeeth and produced by UV Creations. The film stars Pawan Kalyan in the lead role, along with Sarah-Jane Dias, Anjali Lavania, Jackie Shroff, Adivi Sesh, and Atul Kulkarni in pivotal roles. The film's music is composed by Thaman S.
