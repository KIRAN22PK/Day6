ABOUT

RRR is a 2022 Indian Telugu-language epic action drama film directed by S. S. Rajamouli. The film stars N. T. Rama Rao Jr. and Ram Charan in the lead roles, with Alia Bhatt and Ajay Devgn appearing in pivotal roles. The film's music is composed by M. M. Keeravani.
