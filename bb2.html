ABOUT

Baahubali 2: The Conclusion is a 2017 Indian Telugu-language epic action film directed by S. S. Rajamouli. It is the sequel to the 2015 film Baahubali: The Beginning and features an ensemble cast including Prabhas, Rana Daggubati, Anushka Shetty, and Tamannaah. The film's music is composed by M. M. Keeravani.
